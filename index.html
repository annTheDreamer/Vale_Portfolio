Have a look at some of my work

Tasty Box

While working on Tasty Box my focus was on creating an intuitive and streamlined navigation system that empowers users to effortlessly navigate through the platform. By eliminating unnecessary steps and reducing cognitive load, I aimed to make the gifting experience enjoyable and stress-free.

Sugarwish

Sugarwish presents a novel concept in gifting, allowing recipients to choose the specific content of their gift. This level of customization adds a touch of magic, and the goal was to enhance that magic by optimizing the ordering process.

Special Olympics N. Macedonia

Introducing a powerful website designed to elevate non-governmental organizations by providing them with a strong digital presence. This platform serves as a catalyst for increased outreach, showcasing the organization's impactful activities, highlighting the achievements of athletes, and fostering connections to attract vital donations.
